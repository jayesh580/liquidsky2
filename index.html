WHAT WE DO

At LiquidSky we aim to provide sustainable solutions for a purer plant. Water and air are the two major components of our environment. While water is a limited resource that is easily contaminated, rendering it useless. Air is a life-sustaining resource that leads to severe complications when polluted.

Water is fundamental to long-term development and environmental sustainability.

Wastewater is produced by all industries that use water. This untreated wastewater was released directly into the environment and had a detrimental impact over time. With technological advancements, wastewater is gaining momentum as a dependable alternative source of water, shifting the wastewater management paradigm from ‘treatment and disposal' to ‘reuse, recycle, and resource recovery.'

We at LiquidSky provide sustainable solutions to recycle, reuse and resource water. We provide solutions such as vacuum distillation, concentrators, and evaporators to recycle and reuse industrial wastewater, and we provide advanced oxidation for high COD-contaminated water.

Air filtration is mostly required in industries as it helps remove air pollutants and improve indoor air quality. Clean air is a must for the food and beverage industry to maintain hygiene and quality compliance. At LiquidSky we not only provide energy-efficient and environment-friendly exhaust air filter systems but also emphasise on reducing CO2 emissions

KMA FILTERS

KMA Umwelttechnik GmbH, specialies in exhaust air filtration technology for food industry. We emphasise on energy efficient and environment friendly exhaust air filter systems that reduces CO2 emissions while simultaneously recovering energy for the manufacturing industry.
KMA Hybrid Filter is used for the air filtration of industrial frying, smokehouses, spray dryers and cooking systems.
While KMA ULTRAVENT air filtering system guarantees that exhaust air is purified to a high degree, it also recovers the heat contained in the air flow. The energy recovered might be used to heat the air supply or service water. For drying and roasting plants, KMA offers custom-made systems.
The devices purify the exhaust air from food and pet food drying and roasting. They also have modules for heat recovery and odour elimination.
KAM filtrationsystem considerably reduces energy consumption by more than 80%. Aside from the significant energy savings, it also reduces CO2 emissions, ensuring great ecological relevance.
